Frequently Asked Questions

How do subscription plans work?

Choose a plan (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly), book a free consultation, and we'll confirm pricing based on your home size. Once you're set up, your dedicated cleaning team arrives on schedule — no need to rebook each time.

Can I change my plan or skip a cleaning?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or pause your plan at any time. Need to skip a week? Just let us know 48 hours in advance and we'll reschedule — no penalty.

What's included in a standard cleaning?

Every visit covers your kitchen (appliance surfaces, counters, floors), bathrooms (toilet, sink, tub/shower, mirrors, floors), and living areas (dusting, vacuuming, floor washing). Extras like window washing, appliance deep cleaning, and closet interiors are available on request.

Do I get the same cleaning team every time?

Yes. We assign a dedicated team to your home and never share staff between clients. You'll see the same trusted professionals every visit.

How do ScrubbyPts™ work?

Every visit earns ScrubbyPts™. Weekly subscribers earn 2 points per visit, bi-weekly earn 1.5, and monthly earn 1. At 5 points, redeem a free extra service — window washing, laundry room, appliance deep clean, and more. The more often we clean, the faster you earn.

Do you offer services for property managers?

Yes! We provide dedicated cleaning teams per property, volume pricing for portfolios, and turnover cleanings on demand. One point of contact manages all your units. Use the "Let's Talk" button above to get started.
